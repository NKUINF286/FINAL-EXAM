We are always looking for ways to improve

Inspirited by the Sisters of Mercy, we promote the empowerment of individuals and families through programs that address their immediate needs, foster self-reliance, promote holistic health and advocate for social justice. Your comments are important to our work.

Our primary service area is Walnut Hills and East Walnut Hills in Cincinnati, Ohio.
