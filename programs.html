Senior Outreach Services

Mercy Neighborhood Ministries provides support to low income seniors, age 60 and older, who live in zip codes 45206, 45207, and 45212.

Advocacy

Support in managing personal finances

Assistance in completing complicated agency forms and applications

Help seniors with recertification processes for housing, benefits, etc

Decreasing Isolation

One-on-One home visits and phone calls Social Events for birthdays and holidays
